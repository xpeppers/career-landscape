Career Landscape

Why Landscape

We believe that people knowledge is best represented as a landscape, with hills and valley, peaks and depression, more than a ledger like is usualy represented. The idea of a landscape is more linked to a non linear path (and in some way random path) of knowledge development, instead of a linear predefined path, suitable for everyone.
We want a tool to have a discussion on how the person feels are is strong point and is weakness, and in which topics he wants to invest time and energy in the following month.
